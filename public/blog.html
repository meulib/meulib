A simple voice talking to and of God

This book came into my life two months ago. A friend of mine gifted it to me. In it I discovered a voice talking of God and life in a extremely simple human genuine manner. That simple, genuine voice took me in. For some days it was my pleasant companion to relax with for a while after lunch.

In a thin book, author Eddie Askew muses about some common aspect of life, followed by a poem-prayer related to that musing. Each musing is given a title that is a section of The Bible. I have not read The Bible, but that did not prevent the reader from enjoying Eddie's voice and mind.
